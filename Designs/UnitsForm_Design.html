How this form works:

  • 1 Click a building on the left → units grid on the right loads automatically
  • 2 Click "Add Unit" → detail panel appears at the bottom, empty, ready to fill
  • 3 Click a unit row then "Edit Unit" → detail panel fills with that unit's data
  • 4 Check "Has Tenant" → tenant fields become enabled. Uncheck → they grey out
  • 5 Search box searches across ALL buildings by name, owner, phone
  • 6 Delete Unit → soft delete (goes to Deleted Records, data stays safe)
  • 7 Add Building → small input appears to type building name and category
